  • iLLuminate

  • Who we are

    iLLuminate is an extremely selective and competitive program.

    The primary objective of iLLuminate is to provide knowledge, contacts and resources to qualified individuals who are currently enrolled as students of one of the programs under STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics). The guidance is provided in-person, by qualified individuals- who were recent STEM graduates and have acclaimed reputation and executive level titles in Financial Services on Wall Street.

    The program targets at providing such qualified candidates with the best of knowledge and aptitude that prepares and allows individuals to gain a niche and successfully launch their professional career on Wall Street.

  • Who can apply?

    Currently enrolled students, or recent graduates (B.Sc./ B.Tech./ M.Sc./ M.Tech.) from one of the majors under Science, Technology, Engineering and/ or Mathematics (S.T.E.M.). The candidate may or may not have any work experience, but should be highly interested in pursuing a career in the Financial Services area.

  • If I am selected, what does the program require?

    Selection to the program is highly competitive. Once selected, you will be notified and required to deposit a non-refundable processing fee of $95.00. Besides the $95.00 fee, there is absolutely no cost for attending this program. The program will be spread over 4 classes. Each class will be conducted at the NYU-Polytechnic Brooklyn campus. You are required to attend the classes in person. There is no online program available at this time. If you miss a class, you miss the module. There are no make-up sessions.

  • Who will teach me?

    The program provides you with a mentor/ coaches who are S.T.E.M. graduates, established as a senior executive at top financial services firms/ banking organizations on Wall Street. (See profiles for example coaches) Your coaches are not only your teachers, but contacts and reference points for jobs and careers on Wall Street.

  • How long is the program for?

    The program is conducted over 4 sessions, each 90 minutes long, including ‘speed-networking’ sessions, allowing you to interact with coaches.

  • How does this benefit me?

    As a recent graduate/ current student of STEM, the program gears you towards/ provides you with the following: - In class coaching of 360 minutes (4 X 90) provided by highly successful individuals who were themselves a student from one of your programs and are established executives on Wall Stret today.

    - Develop skills and understand what is required from you (intellect, personality, skills etc) for a job on Wall Street. - Strengthen your skills by developing your skills by learning what to do, and more importantly, what not to do.

    - Your mentors are your opportunity to develop contacts for near future. They have hired 10+ candidates from the STEM programs already, and these hires are currently successfully employed at top firms on Wall Street. You could be their next prodigy!

    - Your mentors and coaches will share their real life experiences with you, not text book material. You are free to ask any and all questions to help you understand how they did what they did. What they did day one on the job and what they do today.
